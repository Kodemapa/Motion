About Motion

Motion Education in Kota, India, is a top coaching institute known for excellence in preparing students for NEET, IIT JEE (M+A), Olympiads, Boards, and KVPY. They provide both offline and online courses with fees ranging from 20,000 INR to 4,45,000 INR. Scholarships, based on board exam performance and the Motion Open Scholarship Test (MOST), are available.

Our Success Story

Motion Kota is a coaching institute for competitive exams, founded by Nitin Vijay, an IIT BHU alumnus, in 2007. It uses technology to deliver online and offline courses and has produced many top rankers. It is also the inspiration for the Netflix series Kota Factory
